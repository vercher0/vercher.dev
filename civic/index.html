Civic & Public Service

I am involved in local government and public service with a focus on responsible decision-making, transparency, and long-term community well-being. My civic work emphasizes systems thinking, public accountability, and the practical consequences of policy and governance choices.

I serve in appointed roles within local government and community institutions, supporting responsible governance, ethical oversight, and long-term planning. Approach

My approach to public service is grounded in listening, preparation, and systems thinking. I aim to understand how policies and decisions affect people over time, not just in the moment, and to weigh tradeoffs openly when outcomes are uncertain or contested.
