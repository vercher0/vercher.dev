About

I work on systems — technical, organizational, and civic — that shape how people experience care, governance, and daily life.

What I Work On

My professional work sits at the intersection of healthcare operations, data and analytics, and public accountability. I focus on designing and improving systems that need to be both technically sound and socially trustworthy.

This includes healthcare analytics and business intelligence, infection prevention and patient safety, and governance work in local government and community institutions.

Background

I began my career in military medicine and later moved into nursing, infection prevention, and health system leadership. Over time, my work has increasingly focused on data, systems design, and the ways complex organizations make decisions under uncertainty.

Alongside my professional work, I’ve remained active in civic service, serving on boards and commissions and working on issues related to planning, ethics, and public trust.

  • Consulting — professional systems and analytics work
  • Writing — essays, experiments, and longer reflections
